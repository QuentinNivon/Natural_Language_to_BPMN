The initial task is to IdentifyInventoryStock, if the stock is available, proceed with LoadingOfStock, if it is unavailable, notify the InventoryAvailability service. Once the LoadingOfStock is complete, perform UpdateInventory and NotifyShipment tasks. Then, choose one or many transport options from LandService, AirService, and CargoService. Transport the shipment and NotifyProvider once the CompleteDelivery is finalised.
