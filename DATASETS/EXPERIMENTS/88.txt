The washing process begins by FillWashingMachineWithDirtyClothes. Then the user ChooseCleaningProgram and ChooseDuration. Finally, he SelectWashingTemperature and ProgramStartTime. The machine CleanClothes and then RinsesClothes and SpinClothes. Once done, it MakeNoise to let the user know it is finished. The user therefore StopMachine, CollectClothes and HangClothesToDry. Once dried, he FoldClothesForStorage.
