A customer BringsDefectiveComputer and the CRS CheckDefect and HandsOutRepairCost.
If the customer decides that the costs are acceptable, the process continues.
Otherwise, she takes her computer home unrepaired.
The ongoing repair consists of two activities, which are executed, in any arbitrary order.
The first activity is CheckAndRepairHardware, whereas the second activity CheckAndConfigureSoftware.
After each of these activities, the repairer TestProperSystemFunctionality.
If an error is detected, another arbitrary repair activity is executed.
Otherwise, the repair is finished.
