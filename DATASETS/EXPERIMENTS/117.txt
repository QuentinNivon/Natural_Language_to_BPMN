After a claim is registered, the claims officer ExamineClaim.
The claim officer then WriteSettlementRecommendation.
A senior claim officer then CheckRecommendation.
Depending on the results of the check, he either MarkClaimAsOK or MarkClaimAsNotOK.
If the claim is marked as not ok, the senior officer SendBackClaim, and the original officer must WriteSettlementRecommendation again.
If the claim is ok, the process ends.
