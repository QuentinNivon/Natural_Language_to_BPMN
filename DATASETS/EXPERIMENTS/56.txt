The recruiter start by OpenPosition.
Then, he Publish the offer, DistributeOnJobBoards, and AdvertiseOnSocialMedia.
After that, he eventually RetrieveApplications, which leads to PreSelectCandidates.
Next, he InterviewPreSelectedCandidates.
If a candidate was selected after the interview process, he SelectCandidateForOffer, PrepareOffer, SendOfferToCandidate and GetCandidateReply one after the other.
Otherwise, he RetrieveApplications again.
Once he got the candidate reply, he either CongratulateCandidate and InformOtherCandidates if the answer is positive, or SelectCandidateForOffer again if the answer is negative.
