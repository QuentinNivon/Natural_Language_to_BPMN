First, the user AppliesOnline.
Then, the user has to UploadPassport so that the system can CheckValidity.
If the passport is invalid, the user must UploadPassport again.
Otherwise, the company can EvaluateApplication.
The evaluation leads either to RejectApplication or AcceptApplication.
If the company accepts the application, its administrative staff must PayForFees and PreparePassport.
Once the passport is ready, the company DeliverPassport.
