First, the person ChooseItems.
Then, she has to either PayByMealCard, PayByCash, or PayByCreditCard.
If she pays by meal card but has no money left, she has to RechargeCard before PayByMealCard again.
If she pays by cash, the cashier must ReturnChange if any.
Finally, if she pays by credit card, the system VerifiesPayment and if the payment failed she has to PayByCreditCard again.
