First, the administration has to give the MissionAuthorization.
Then, while the user BookFlight, the administrative staff can do the MissionPaperwork.
After booking his flight, the user is allowed to BookHotel, TakeInsurance, CheckLocalTransportation, and DoVaccination.
Once he has booked the hotel and is done with his mission paperwork, he can start the VisaProcess.
In the end, the administration ArchiveMission.
