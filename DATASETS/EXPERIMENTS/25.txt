The evanstonian is an upscale independent hotel. When a guest CallRoomService at the evanstonian, the room-service manager TakeDownOrder. She then SubmitOrderTick to the kitchen to begin PrepareFood. She also GiveOrderToSommlier (i.e., the wine waiter) to FetchWineFromCellar and PrepareOtherAlcoholicBeverages. Eighty percent of room-service orders include wine or some other alcoholic beverage. Finally, she AssignOrderToWaiter. While the kitchen and the sommelier are doing their tasks, the waiter ReadiesCart (i.e., puts a tablecloth on the cart and gathers silverware). The waiter is also responsible of PrepareNonAlcoholicDrinks. Once the food, wine, and cart are ready, the waiter DeliverFoodWineAndCarte to the guests room. After returning to the room-service station, the waiter DebitsGuestAccount. The waiter may WaitForBilling if he has another order to prepare or deliver.
