The process of vacations request starts when any employee of the organization StartsVacationRequest.
Once he has finished his request, he can SubmitsVacationRequest.
Once the requirement is registered, the immediate supervisor of the employee requesting the vacation ReceiveRequest.
The supervisor must ApproveRequest or RejectRequest.
If the request is rejected, the system ReturnApplicationToApplicant who can ReviewRejectionReasons and SubmitsVacationRequest again.
If the request is approved the system GenerateNotificationToHumanResources, whose representative must CompleteManagementProcedures.
