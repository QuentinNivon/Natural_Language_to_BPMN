Purchase Order Process Task 1: PlaceOrder - A customer places an order through the companys website. Task 2: CheckInventory - The system checks the inventory for availability. Task 3: GenerateInvoice - If the item is in stock, the system automatically generates an invoice. Task 4: NotifyWarehouse - Notifies the warehouse to dispatch the item. Task 5: ContactCustomer - If the item is not available, the customer service team contacts the customer to discuss alternative options or a refund.
